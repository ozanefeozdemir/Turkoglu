SHIPYARD INFORMATION
•We have been continuing our shipyard experience for more 
than 60 years, on an area of   20 decares in Yalova since 2007, 
after the Golden Horn and Tuzla.
•In our shipyard, we can buildships up to 4,000 dwt and small-
scale boats such as fishermen, passengers, yachts, tugboats 
and barges for maintenance and repair.
•In addition, we have the capacity to build various ships up to
20.000 dwt as new builds, as well as all kinds of steel or 
aluminum marine vehicles.
